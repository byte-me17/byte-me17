Christmas Recipes

Stuck for ideas but don't want to make the standard Christmas dishes? Check out some of the recipes below!

Christmas Crackers
These festive crackers, with their delicious chicken, mushroom and broccoli filling, and crisp filo casing, make an excellent lighter choice for a dinner party.

Brie and Cranberry Melts
Toasted baguette, creamy brie and tart cranberries—that’s all you need to pull together these fancy appetizers. We guarantee no one will believe you when you tell them how easy they are to make.

Spinach and Gruyere Potato Casserole
This bake makes an easy and delicious vegetarian dinner with a robust green salad on the side. For a heartier meal, eat it as a side with your Christmas roast.

Pistachio-Roasted Spiced Carrots
These Roasted Carrots with Pistachio will make any vegetable naysayer a convert. With a sweet honey glaze, these are the perfect side to your festive meal.

Santa Claus Brownie Hats
These bite-size chocolate brownies topped with strawberry Santa hats make for a fun and festive sweet treat. Great for all you big kids out there!

Reindeer Marshmallow Pops
Complete with pretzel antlers, edible eyes and a red candy nose. Because who doesn’t love a dessert that does double duty as tabletop décor?

Gingerbread Eggnog Hot Chocolate
Chase away the winter chill with this fun twist on hot chocolate that combines eggnog and gingerbread flavours into one unbelievably-cozy sipper!

Peppermint Chocolate Latte
Indulge yourself with this delicious peppermint chocolate coffee drink. Perfect for festive holidays and on dark winter nights (and mornings).

Mulled Wine
Eat, drink and be merry with the traditional festive tipple. It's like Chirstmas in a glass.

Candy Cane Cocktail
Got a bit of a sweet tooth? Well, December is finally here - let’s start celebrating with this Candy Cane Martini!
